Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe for an easy no-bake skillet lasagna right on the stove top.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Chopped Onions
  • Clove of Garlic
  • Dried Basil
  • Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned. Drain grease and then add seasoning and sauce while cooking on low heat.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil. Cook noodles until tender.
  3. Add cooked and drained noodles to sauce. Sprinkle cheese while cooking
  4. Place skillet into hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly.
