Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe for a delicious burger with egg, bacon, and avacados.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Buns
  • Bacon
  • Ground Beef
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Steak Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Lettuce
  • Avacado

Steps

  1. Preheat grill with oil and place butter buns it.
  2. Place bacon in a skillet and cook over medium-high heat.
  3. Mix ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, and steak seasoning in a large bowl. Make patties after mixing.
  4. Grill patties on the grill until preferred doneness.
  5. Crack eggs on grill and cook until preferred doneness.
  6. Assemble burger with all the ingredients and enjoy.
