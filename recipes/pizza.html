Pizza

Description

This is the recipe for a quick and easy thin-crust pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dry Yeast
  • White Sugar
  • Lukewarm Water
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeat and sugar in hot water in a bowl. Let stand for 5-8 minutes.
  2. Mix 1 3/4 cup flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in yeast mixture and mix well.
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth. Roll into a circle.
  4. Put sauce, toppings, and cheese of choice and enjoy.
